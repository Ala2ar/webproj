Our Story

Committed to excellence in women's healthcare since 2005

Our Journey

2005

Founded by Dr. Barnes Reynolds with a vision to provide comprehensive women's healthcare in a compassionate environment.

2010

Expanded our facility to include state-of-the-art surgical suites and advanced diagnostic equipment.

2015

Recognized as a Center of Excellence in Minimally Invasive Gynecology by the American Association of Gynecologic Laparoscopists.

2020

Launched our Maternal-Fetal Medicine program to serve high-risk pregnancies in the region.

Today

Continuing to innovate and provide exceptional care with a team of 12 specialists across multiple women's health disciplines.
